Tethered Together

Tethered Together is an innovative movement-based game inspired by the concept of "chained together" mechanics. The players are bound to one another with a tether and must use this vibrant connection to climb walls, swing across gaps, and solve puzzles as a group. Cooperative play is encouraged in the game where the coordination and precise timing of the players are crucial to proceed through the levels. Presently, the movement system is being built, including wall climbing, tether mechanics, and basic environmental interaction. Not yet finished, the project's aim is to refine these mechanics further, with more advanced terrain, obstacles, and team-oriented challenges being worked on. This project illustrates my movement system, physics-based game experience, and collaboration mechanics abilities with an emphasis on creating rich and engaging player interactions. This project is a highly faithful reproduction of Dust II, arguably the most iconic map from Counter-Strike 2 / CS:GO, built from the ground up in Roblox. The focus of this project is on realism, performance optimization, and environmental accuracy. Every corridor, bombsite, and line of sight has been accurately recreated to provide players with a realistic experience. Future patches will enhance lighting, textures, and interactive objects to make it even more realistic. This project demonstrates my skills in level design, optimization, and gameplay mechanics, and as such, it is a great example of my ability to produce high-quality FPS environments in Roblox.
